The Rae Burket Mission

Rae Burket Shoes was founded in 2005 by Mirela Rae Burket. Mirela was inspired after serving two years in Romania as a member of the Peace Corps where she witnessed poverty, as well as many children running through the streets barefoot. That’s when she recognized a simple, yet revolutionary solution – shoes. She quickly set out to build a company in the U.S. market with a simple goal: to show how, together, we cantake action today to create a better tomorrow.
